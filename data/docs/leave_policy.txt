Leave and Attendance Policy
HR Department
Version: 2.0

LEAVE TYPES
===========
1. Annual Leave:
   - 18 days per year
   - Carry forward: Maximum 5 days
   - Apply minimum 2 days in advance

2. Sick Leave:
   - 10 days per year
   - Medical certificate required for 3+ consecutive days
   - Cannot be carried forward

3. Emergency Leave:
   - 3 days per year
   - Call HR immediately: hr@company.com
   - Submit application within 24 hours

4. Work From Home:
   - Maximum 2 days per week
   - Manager approval required
   - Must be available on all communication channels

HOW TO APPLY LEAVE
==================
Step 1: Login to HR portal: hr.company.com
Step 2: Click on Leave Management
Step 3: Select leave type
Step 4: Select dates
Step 5: Add reason
Step 6: Submit for approval
Step 7: Manager approves within 2 working days

ATTENDANCE RULES
================
- Office timing: 9 AM to 6 PM
- Flexible timing: 8 AM to 8 PM (core hours 11-4)
- Late coming: More than 3 times = warning
- Half day: If leaving before 1 PM or arriving after 1 PM
