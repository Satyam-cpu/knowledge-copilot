VPN Setup and Troubleshooting Guide
Version: 1.5
Last Updated: February 2026

WHAT IS VPN?
VPN (Virtual Private Network) allows you to securely
access company resources from anywhere.

SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
==================
Step 1: Download Cisco AnyConnect
  - Go to: vpn.company.com/download
  - Select your operating system
  - Download and install

Step 2: Configure AnyConnect
  - Open Cisco AnyConnect
  - Enter server: vpn.company.com
  - Click Connect

Step 3: Authentication
  - Enter your company email
  - Enter your password
  - Enter MFA code from authenticator app
  - Click OK

TROUBLESHOOTING
===============
Problem 1: Cannot connect to VPN
  Solution: Check internet connection first
  Solution: Restart AnyConnect application
  Solution: Try different network

Problem 2: MFA code not working
  Solution: Check time on your phone is correct
  Solution: Re-sync authenticator app
  Solution: Contact IT for MFA reset

Problem 3: VPN connects but cannot access resources
  Solution: Disconnect and reconnect VPN
  Solution: Clear browser cache
  Solution: Contact IT helpdesk
